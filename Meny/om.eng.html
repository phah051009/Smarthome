About me

My name is Philip Ahlqvist and I am in the second year of the technology program at LBS Kreativa Gymnasiet in Växjö. Beyond school I work on IV Product with control and regulation systems. My main work at IV Produkt involves customer support where I help customers connect their facilities to IV Produkt's cloud service. In my spare time I travel a lot and invents funny things.

The mart Home project is about creating a blog website with a focus of your choice. The blog site shall include a home page, about me page, blog page, posts with images and text, and a contact page. The project requires a lot of planning of color theme and structure. It was also important to collect material which may be displayed on the website, such as images, and texts. Finally, after the website is finished, I will write a report about the project.
