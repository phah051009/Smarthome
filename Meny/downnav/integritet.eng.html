Smart Home Privacy Policy

We at Smart Home value your privacy and take it seriously. That is why we have developed this privacy policy to describe how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you using our products and services.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from you when you use our webshop and blog, such as name, address, email address, phone number and password. This information helps us improve our products and services and to provide a better user experience.

Protection of Personal Information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access access, use, alteration or loss. We use security protocols to protect your information below transmission and storage.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of or limitation of your personal information. You also have the right to object to our use of your information for direct marketing purposes. If you want to exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@smarthome.com.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time.
