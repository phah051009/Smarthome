Verisure Smart Voice Alarm: Large, flexible and easy to use

My rating: 4 out of 5 stars

Verisure's alarms have developed a lot in the past year and several parts we previously mentioned have fixed. This is Sweden's best home alarm right now.

Verisure offers a number of different packages depending on your needs, and unlike many competitors they offer both free calls and professional installation and review of the system. You need simply just watch as everything installs which is convenient, although it's also a reason to the system costs a lot.

Expandable with smart accessories

The package we start from in the test is called Smart Voice Alarm and consists of the basic components you expect from a modern alarm. With great possibilities to expand. New for this year is the new one the alarm panel. It is considerably larger than its predecessor, but then it also has a built-in siren and speakers too two-way communication with Verisure. So you don't have to plug in a separate voice unit.

It is of course possible to get away cheaper, but the supplier does not have an open price list on the site, without the would rather contact you to negotiate an offer depending on how and where you live - as well as what needs you have. We think it's a bit ugly, but Verisure is not alone in that arrangement.

In addition to the gadgets in the basic package, we have tested a large number of extra accessories in the form of IP cameras (for indoor and outdoor use), Yale Doorman module, remote switch (smartplug), security panel, climate sensor and leak sensor.

The possibilities to expand the system are almost endless and all functions work really well. With You never need to alarm the Yale module - it is done automatically when you unlock the door lock. We tested the integration with the latest Yale Doorman L3 and the lock works much easier in Verisure's system than when you using Yale software.

By combining sensors from your various devices, you can set up a smart home there, for example the element in the friggebod starts up when it approaches freezing temperatures. Extremely flexible! We tested to and with controlling a terrarium (temperature and humidity) by using a climate sensor (i the terrarium) in combination with three different remote switches connected to the heating lamp, heating loop and humidifier. The possibilities are huge when you set up automations, even if the actual overview of yours macros quickly become hard to grasp and after a while it's easy to forget how you set yours lights, what happens when the sun goes down and what your schedule looks like for your smart lock. Here are surface space for improvement in the interface.

Arlo cameras provide added value

For some time now, Arlo's outdoor cameras have been used in Verisure's system. We have tested several different Arlo cameras for a long time and they work perfectly with the same functionality as from a regular one Arlo account. However, not all models are supported if you intend to connect existing cameras to your alarm account. For example Arlo Go 2 has not yet received support. However, you can now use Arlo Pro 4, which gives a very good image and functionality. You also don't have to pay for Arlo's extra services, these are included in the purchase when you connects the cameras to the Verisure account.

The interface of the Verisure app is clean and clear, you can quickly see the status of your system. Here you can also see information such as temperature, humidity, and many other useful things if you bought more other smart accessories. Perfect winter time to get an overview of the indoor climate.

A relatively new feature in the app is Verisure Guardian. Here, wherever you are, you can use the app as a contact alarm in the mobile phone. On the one hand, there is a built-in SOS service, and on the other hand you can set up when you expect you stay at home with the Check on me function. If you are not home in time, the contact alarm is activated and sent your last known position to the alarm center.

We tested the service and when the timer counts down to zero, Verisure calls repeatedly and speaks out in the speaker unit at home to see if everything is okay. At the same time, guards are sent to the mobile's position. The there is also the option to activate a silent alarm, a function that is good to have if you are exposed, for example for domestic violence but do not want to reveal that you sent an alarm.

Verisure's alarm system can easily cost you a fortune if you go all in, but it's also more than just a standard home alarm. Recommended!
