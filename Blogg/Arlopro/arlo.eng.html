Arlo launches new IP camera and updates the app

Can film high-definition evidence in color even when it's dark outside.

Arlo has now launched its new security camera Arlo Pro 5. The new camera can record color video in 2k resolution even in the dark to collect material that can be used as evidence and lead to a conviction in case a burglar is present.

The camera also has a zoom function of up to twelve times which should facilitate taking so-called "mugshots" at an intruder when the floodlight, which reaches up to seven meters, is turned on. The camera also has one built-in siren.

Arlo Pro 5 can handle up to eight months of operation on battery life. There is also a new low power mode which can provide an additional 50 percent longer battery life. Arlo Pro 5 is also the first camera from the company with Dual-Band Wifi with support for both the 2.4 and 5.0 GHz frequency bands.

It will also be the first Arlo camera to launch with the new Arlo Secure app. The new app's "One-Tap" technology should enable faster navigation and easier monitoring of the home. It should too are expanded possibilities for personalization for greater control over the security of the home.

Arlo Pro 5 is available to buy on April 1, 2023. Recommended price is SEK 2,790.
